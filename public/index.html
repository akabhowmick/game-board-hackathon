How to Play Ludo

Game Objective

Move all four of your tokens from the base to home by traveling around the board. The first player to get all four tokens home wins!

Basic Rules

  • Taking Turns: Players take turns clockwise, starting with the red player.
  • Rolling the Dice: Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll a six-sided die.
  • Moving Out of Base: You need to roll a 6 to move a token out of your base.
  • Moving Tokens: Click on a token to move it the number of spaces shown on the dice.
  • No Valid Moves: If you cannot make any valid moves, your turn passes automatically.

Movement Rules

  • Path: Each color follows their own path around the board before entering their home stretch.
  • Multiple Tokens: You can choose which token to move if you have multiple options.
  • Exact Count: You must have the exact count to reach home. If you roll too high, you cannot move that token.

Capturing Opponent Tokens

  • If your token lands on a cell occupied by an opponent's token, their token is sent back to their base.
  • You cannot capture tokens on safe cells (marked with stars on the board).
  • Multiple tokens of the same color can occupy the same cell.

Special Rules

  • Rolling a 6: Gives you another turn after moving.
  • Safe Zones: Your home stretch is safe from captures by other players.

Winning the Game

  • You win by moving all four tokens to your home.
  • The game continues until all but one player has completed the game, establishing a ranking.

Controls

  • Roll Dice: Click the dice button.
  • Move Token: Click on the token you want to move after rolling.
